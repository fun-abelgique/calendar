BONUS DAYS!!!




If you add up the number of days in all 13 months above, you get 364 days. However, there are 365 days in a year. To fix this, a bonus day starts every year in Calendar Factorial. The bonus days normally occur on March 20 on the Gregorian calendar. On years in Calendar Factorial that begin during leap years on the Gregorian calendar, there are 2 bonus days to start off a year.
