This is the year. Calendar!, according to itself, started in 2000 for simplicity, but it was created in 2022 and first used in 2023 (those were all Gregorian calendar years).

And finally..

C! (Calendar!) is the equivalent of CE (Common Era) or AD (Anno Domini), and dates before March 20, 2000, are marked BC! (Before Calendar!), which is the equivalent of BCE (Before Common Era) or BC (Before Christ).
